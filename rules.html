Rules & Regulations

White Eagles teams participate in youth and adult leagues that each have their own official rules.

Youth Rules (CMSA)

Youth programs at White Eagles are registered through Calgary Minor Soccer Association (CMSA). All youth league games follow CMSA rules and regulations.

CMSA Rules & Regulations

For full details on age groups, field sizes, game formats, and discipline, please refer to CMSA’s official rules.

View CMSA Rules

Adult Rules (CUSA)

Adult White Eagles teams participate in Calgary United Soccer Association (CUSA) competitions. CUSA is responsible for league rules, match procedures, and discipline for adult divisions.

CUSA Rules & Regulations

For the latest adult league rules, please refer to CUSA’s official documentation. This includes competition formats, player eligibility, discipline, and match day regulations.
