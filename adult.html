Adult Soccer at White Eagles

White Eagles adult teams provide a pathway for motivated players to continue their soccer journey beyond youth ages. The focus is on strong competition, team culture, and representing the club at the adult level.

Adult teams compete in Calgary United Soccer Association (CUSA) competitions. Match rules and league regulations are governed by CUSA. For full adult league rules, please refer directly to the CUSA website.

Join an Adult Team

Adult soccer is open to players aged 18+ who are committed to training and match attendance. Experience playing youth club, school, or adult recreational soccer is recommended.

If you are interested in joining or trialing with an adult team, please contact the club so we can match you with the most suitable team and season.
